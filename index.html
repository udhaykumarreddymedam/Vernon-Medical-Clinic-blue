In an emergency? Need help now?

At our medical clinic, your health and safety are our top priorities. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please don't hesitate to seek immediate assistance. Call --- or visit the nearest emergency room if you have severe symptoms such as chest pain, difficulty breathing, sudden weakness or numbness, heavy bleeding, or any other serious condition. For urgent, but non-life-threatening issues, our clinic offers same-day appointments and walk-in services to ensure you receive prompt care when you need it most. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is here to provide the support and treatment you require to get back on your feet quickly and safely.

Cardiology

We are dedicated to helping you maintain a healthy heart through personalized treatment plans and preventative care strategies.

At Vernon Medical Clinic, our Cardiology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating a wide range of heart conditions. Our experienced cardiologists use state-of-the-art technology to provide comprehensive care, including cardiac screenings, echocardiograms, and stress tests. We are dedicated to helping you maintain a healthy heart through personalized treatment plans and preventative care strategies.

Neurology

We are committed to improving your quality of life with compassionate and tailored medical care.

Our Neurology Department offers expert care for patients with neurological disorders, including migraines, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis. Our team of skilled neurologists employs advanced diagnostic tools and therapeutic techniques to manage and treat complex neurological conditions. We are committed to improving your quality of life with compassionate and tailored medical care.

Hepatology

We focus on holistic care, combining medical treatments with lifestyle modifications to ensure optimal liver health.

The Hepatology Department at Vernon Medical Clinic provides specialized care for liver-related diseases such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and fatty liver disease. Our hepatologists are equipped with the latest knowledge and technology to offer accurate diagnoses and effective treatment options. We focus on holistic care, combining medical treatments with lifestyle modifications to ensure optimal liver health.

Pediatrics

We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for both children and their families, ensuring that your child's health is our top priority.

Our Pediatrics Department is dedicated to the health and well-being of children from birth through adolescence. Our pediatricians offer a full spectrum of services, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, growth and development assessments, and treatment for common childhood illnesses. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for both children and their families, ensuring that your child's health is our top priority.

Frequently Asked Questioins

  • How can I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment by calling our reception at [insert phone number] during business hours. Alternatively, you can use our online booking system available on our website.

  • Is the clinic accessible for disabled patients?

    Yes, our clinic is fully accessible for disabled patients. We have a designated disabled car parking space and a downstairs surgery that can be specifically requested when making an appointment.

  • Can I get a medical certificate for work or school?

    Yes, we provide medical certificates for work, school, pre-employment, and adoption medicals. Please inform the doctor during your consultation if you need a certificate.

  • How do I get a repeat prescription?

    For repeat prescriptions, you can request them through our online patient portal, call our clinic, or visit us in person. Please allow 48 hours for the prescription to be processed.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and health insurance (if applicable). Please check with our reception for more details on insurance providers we partner with.

  • Do you accept walk-in patients?

    We do accept walk-in patients, but we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you receive timely care.
